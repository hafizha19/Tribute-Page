Oki Setiana Dewi completed her studies at the University of Indonesia, Faculty of Cultural Sciences in 2012. The actress with a Muslim icon has inspired many teenagers in the country. He became the best student and achievers on campus namely as the best new student of OKK UI 2007, the best new student of PSA MABIM FIB UI 2007, as well as an outstanding student in the field of FIB UI 2010. After obtaining his bachelor's degree, Oki became a female student at Tahfidzul Qur'an at Home Qur'an which is located in Depok. Then, he studied Arabic at Umm Al Qura University in Makkah in 2012. In the same year, his love of education and children, Oki continued his postgraduate studies in the field of Early Childhood Education (PAUD) at Jakarta State University (Jakarta State University ( UNJ).
